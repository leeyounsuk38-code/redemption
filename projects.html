Projects

Here are our humanitarian projects by Redemption

1. Future Elite Team (F.E.T)

A growth support program for underprivileged and overlooked students.

Future Elite Team (F.E.T) Plain text Future Elite Team (F.E.T) is a long-term mentorship and support program designed for students who are often overlooked in traditional education systems. Instead of focusing only on top-performing students, this project identifies a small number of underperforming or socially isolated students twice a year through confidential school recommendations. These students receive continuous attention, mentorship, emotional support, and small but meaningful gifts. The program emphasizes human values, dream visualization, and long-term relationship building until adulthood. Core belief: Even the lowest-ranked student today can become a future elite.

2. Global Appliance & Machinery Support

A humanitarian project providing refurbished appliances and farming equipment to developing regions.

Global Appliance & Machinery Support Plain text This humanitarian project collects, repairs, and redistributes used appliances and equipment to developing regions such as Africa, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East. Through partnerships with corporations and recycling organizations, discarded but functional products are restored by skilled technicians and delivered where they are most needed. Essential items include refrigerators, washing machines, water purifiers, air conditioners, and more, with a strong focus on clean water solutions. Core belief: Used technology can create new life opportunities.

3. Mission-Based Academy

An education system focused on faith, humanity, and service.

Mission-Based Academy Plain text Mission-Based Academy is an educational initiative rooted in faith, humanitarian values, and service. Inspired by early missionary contributions and foundational national values, the program focuses on developing character, leadership, and responsibility in young people. Dedicated educational institutions will be established to provide structured learning that combines history, ethics, leadership, and modern skills. Core belief: A strong nation is built on strong values and responsible leaders.

4. Population Recovery & Relationship Support

A social program designed to support relationships and increase birth rates.

Population Recovery & Relationship Support Plain text This project addresses the global population decline crisis by supporting relationships, marriage, and family formation. In collaboration with governments, a structured system will provide marriage incentives, housing support, and financial assistance. A public dating system will be introduced, organizing monthly local gatherings where unmarried individuals can naturally meet and interact. Participants will receive financial support for dating to reduce economic pressure. Core goal: Revive relationships to restore the future of society.

5. Golden Ticket Dream Project

A global game-based system that provides dreams and opportunities for children.

Golden Ticket Dream Project Plain text The Golden Ticket Dream Project is a global participation-based game designed to inspire children and promote positive values. Special coupons are distributed through everyday products, allowing participants to collect, upgrade, and earn rewards through a structured system. The game encourages cooperation, respect, and kindness, while offering opportunities ranging from small gifts to life-changing rewards such as education, travel, and housing. Core belief: Small opportunities can create big dreams and a better future.

6. Zimbabwe Hotel Project

A global hub connecting Africa and Asia through hospitality and care services.

Zimbabwe Hotel Project Plain text The Zimbabwe Hotel Project aims to establish a global hub connecting Africa and Asia. By acquiring and transforming an existing hotel, the project will provide integrated services including hospitality, healthcare, business support, translation, and long-term care. The hotel will operate as a humanitarian-centered facility rather than a profit-driven business, offering significant benefits to African visitors and special groups. Core belief: A hotel can become a bridge between people, cultures, and continents.

7. Community Bank Project

A local financial support system focused on trust and community development.

Community Bank & Financial Support System Plain text This project transforms existing financial institutions into community-centered support systems. By integrating financial services with education and humanitarian values, the system will provide accessible banking, financial guidance, and emergency support for local residents. It also prepares communities for future financial systems while maintaining transparency and trust. Core belief: Finance should serve people, not control them.

8. Driver Welfare & Retirement Support

A welfare system ensuring stability and retirement support for drivers.

Title: Driver Welfare & Retirement Support Project In Korea, “designated drivers” provide a late-night service where they safely drive people home after drinking at social or business gatherings. They work long hours at night, often dealing with intoxicated customers. Their work environment is physically demanding, as they must wait outdoors in extreme weather conditions — hot summers and cold winters — while accepting ride requests through mobile applications. Most of these drivers are ordinary people — fathers, brothers, and family providers — working hard to support their households. Drivers are affiliated with mobile platform companies such as Handle For You, Gogo Driver, and Daeri Driver services. They pay approximately 20% commission fees to these platforms and often endure unfair treatment or difficult working conditions without complaint. Although they contribute to employment insurance, they do not receive retirement benefits. This project aims to provide retirement support for long-term drivers who have worked in this field for over 10 years. - Annual financial support ranging from $1,000 to $10,000 per qualified driver - Recognition of long-term dedication and contribution - Financial stability support for their future “To ensure that unseen and hardworking people are given the dignity and security they deserve.”

Zimbabwe Hotel Project

"Beyond a hotel, connecting people and continents."

Overview

The Zimbabwe Hotel Project aims to establish a global hub by acquiring and transforming an existing hotel into a multi-functional facility that connects Africa and Asia. This project integrates hospitality, healthcare, business, and humanitarian services into one unified platform.

Purpose

  • To serve as a bridge between Africa and Asia
  • To provide integrated services including tourism, business, and medical support
  • To operate on a value-based model focused on people, not just profit

Core Services

  • Translation and business support services
  • Tourism and transportation assistance
  • Medical support (on-site doctors and nurses available 24/7)
  • Long-term stay and care services for elderly residents

Operational Features

  • Partnerships with global hotel brands
  • Professional management system
  • 24-hour medical response system

Benefit Policy

  • Zimbabwe citizens: up to 90% discount
  • African countries: up to 50% discount
  • Children of national contributors: up to 70% discount

Expected Impact

  • Large-scale job creation
  • Activation of African business and tourism
  • Expansion of medical and welfare services
  • Strengthening global cooperation and exchange

Message

"A hotel that connects people, services, and continents."

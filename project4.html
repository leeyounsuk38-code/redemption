Population Recovery & Relationship Support Program

"When relationships begin, the future begins again."

Overview

This program is designed to address the global population decline crisis by supporting natural relationship-building opportunities. By reducing the financial burden of dating and increasing social interaction, we aim to encourage relationships, marriage, and ultimately family formation.

Background

  • Declining marriage rates and birth rates
  • Financial pressure discouraging relationships
  • Lack of social opportunities to meet people
  • Population decline across multiple countries

How It Works

  • Monthly local group meetings organized in public spaces
  • Open participation for single individuals (no strict age limit)

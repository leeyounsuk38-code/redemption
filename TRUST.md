# 🔐 TRUST STRUCTURE (Redemption)

## Overview
This trust structure is designed to support the Redemption ecosystem, including humanitarian projects, family support, and long-term sustainability.

---

## Purpose
- Support global humanitarian projects (Project 1–8)
- Provide financial stability for family and close community
- Ensure long-term and protected asset management
- Enable transparent and responsible fund distribution

---

## Type of Trust
Irrevocable Private Trust (Planned)

This structure is intended to protect assets permanently and ensure they are used according to the mission.

---

## Allocation Strategy
- Project funding (Redemption 1–8)
- Community and family support
- Emergency and welfare funds
- Expansion and infrastructure investment

---

## Governance
- Trustee (professional or institutional)
- Clear rules for fund usage
- Long-term mission-based management

---

## Financial Philosophy
Money is not a tool for profit only, but a tool to support people, stability, and future growth.

---

## Future Plan
- Connect with financial institutions
- Expand into global trust network
- Integrate with digital financial systems (including future QFS concepts)

---

## Note
This structure is conceptual and will require legal consultation before implementation.

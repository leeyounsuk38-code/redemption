Global Appliance & Machinery Support Project

"Used appliances are not the end, but a new beginning."

Overview

This project focuses on collecting, repairing, and redistributing used household appliances and agricultural machinery to developing regions such as the Middle East and Africa. By combining technology and resources, we aim to improve living conditions and support sustainable development.

Background

  • Korea has world-class appliance manufacturing technology
  • A large number of usable second-hand appliances are discarded
  • Developing countries lack essential living and farming infrastructure

How It Works

  • Establish a collection system for used appliances and machinery
  • Repair and quality inspection by skilled technicians
  • Collaboration with companies and recycling organizations
  • Distribution based on local demand and priority needs

Supported Items

  • Washing machines
  • Refrigerators
  • Water purifiers (filters)
  • Televisions
  • Air conditioners
  • Agricultural machinery
  • Bicycles and motorcycles
  • Essential daily equipment

Key Features

  • Humanitarian support based on recycled resources
  • Combination of technology and sustainability
  • International cooperation and shared growth model

Expected Impact

  • Improved living standards and hygiene conditions
  • Increased agricultural productivity
  • Environmental protection through recycling
  • Job creation and skill development

Message

"Technology and resources can create a new life."

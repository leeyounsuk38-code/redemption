Future Elite Team (F.E.T)

"Even those left behind can become future elites."

Overview

Future Elite Team is a humanitarian program designed to support students who are academically struggling, socially isolated, or facing difficult life circumstances. Through continuous care, mentorship, and small but meaningful support, we aim to unlock their hidden potential and guide them toward a better future.

Target

  • Low-performing students
  • Students struggling to adapt to school life
  • Students from difficult family backgrounds
  • Socially isolated or neglected youth

How It Works

  • Selection twice a year (recommended by schools, private process)
  • Small-group focused care (1–2 students per class)
  • Long-term relationship-based support

Core Programs

  • Continuous emotional support and relationship building
  • Regular mentoring sessions
  • Small gifts and daily-life support
  • Character and humanitarian education
  • Dream visualization and goal setting

Principles

  • No labeling or public exposure
  • Focus on long-term growth, not short-term aid
  • Potential-based, not grade-based selection

Expected Impact

  • Restoration of self-esteem
  • Emotional stability
  • Improved school adaptation
  • Clear future direction

Message

"Care can change a life."

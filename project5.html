Golden Ticket Dream Project

"Giving dreams to children, and hope to the world."

Overview

The Golden Ticket Dream Project is a global participation-based system that combines gaming, rewards, and education. By distributing special coupons (“Golden Tickets”) through everyday products, children and participants can collect, upgrade, and unlock opportunities and rewards.

How It Works

  • Golden Tickets are inserted into products such as food, drinks, and school supplies
  • Participants collect and upgrade tickets through missions and activities
  • Online and offline systems are connected for a global experience

Game Structure

  • Basic Ticket → Upgraded Ticket → Premium Ticket
  • Step-by-step missions and events
  • Growth through cooperation, sharing, and challenges

Rewards

  • Small gifts to large-scale rewards
  • Travel tickets
  • Laptops and electronics
  • Scholarships
  • Vehicles and housing opportunities

Key Features

  • Combination of game, education, and opportunity
  • Global participation system
  • Encourages positive values such as cooperation and challenge

Expected Impact

  • Providing dreams and motivation to children
  • Building positive values and mindset
  • Expanding global cultural interaction

Message

"A small ticket can create great joy and a new future."

Driver Welfare & Retirement Support Project

"Even unseen labor deserves a secure future."

Overview

This project focuses on building a welfare and retirement support system for drivers and platform workers who often lack stable income and social security. By creating a structured support system, we aim to ensure long-term stability and a dignified future for essential workers.

Background

  • Unstable income structure for platform-based drivers
  • Lack of retirement benefits and social protection
  • Difficulty in preparing for long-term financial security

How It Works

  • Establish a dedicated retirement savings system for drivers
  • Automatic accumulation based on work activity and duration
  • Collaboration with government, companies, and platforms

Support System

  • Retirement fund accumulation and payout
  • Emergency financial assistance programs
  • Health and safety support
  • Financial education and asset management guidance

Key Features

  • Protection system for non-traditional workers
  • Sustainable and scalable welfare structure
  • Expansion of social safety networks

Expected Impact

  • Improved life stability for drivers
  • Long-term financial security
  • Stronger social trust and recognition of labor value
  • Establishment of a new welfare model for platform workers

Message

"Every form of work deserves dignity and security."
